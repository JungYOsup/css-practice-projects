OSAKA

Osaka City (Osaka City) is a city in Osaka Prefecture, Japan, located in Osaka Bay on the Yodogawa River estuary of Honshu Kinki region. Osaka City is also designated as an ordinance-designated city by the Japanese Local Autonomy Law and is the seat of the Osaka Prefectural Government. Osaka is historically Japan's commercial center and the heart of Japan's second largest gay Hanshin metropolitan area. The weekly population is second only to Tokyo, followed by Japan, followed by Yokohama at night. In 1956, it was designated as the first government designated city in Japan. It is the largest non-Kanto region, and it is a place where many Koreans live together.
